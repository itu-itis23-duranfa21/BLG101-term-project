About Me

I am an undergraduate student in the Department of Computer Engineering at Istanbul Technical University (ITU). I currently do AI research concerning perception and robotics, as part of a TUBITAK project at Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Laboratory (Air Lab) in ITU. I also collaborate in AI research in Brain and Signal Research & Analysis Laboratory (Basira Lab) for medical image analysis. I have a paper in MICCAI 2022 Conference. I've done an internship at Artiwise on NLP research and development for sentiment analysis.
